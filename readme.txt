Add a small "Tweet this" call-to-action box that floats in the bottom right corner of the reader's browser.

== Description ==

Tweet Prompt Box makes it easy for your readers to share your content via Twitter and help drive new visitors to your site.

When a reader scrolls down while reading your post, a small box appears in the bottom right corner of the browser window asking them to tweet the link. You can customize the call to action message as well as choose from a light or dark theme to match your site.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `tweet-prompt-box` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Customize your call to action and choose a theme at `Settings > Tweet Prompt Box`
4. Readers will now see a call to action to share your content
5. Sit back and watch your traffic increase through increased sharing

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does it work on mobile? =

Yes. The prompt box appears fixed to the bottom of the mobile browser window.
